About MangleBracket

MangleBracket was created by John Simons in the spring of 2008. John is from Seattle, WA, and currently lives in Brooklyn, NY.

MangleBracket was developed on a Mac and is hosted on the Google App Engine. DOC and DOCX conversion are handled by OpenOffice 3.2 running on Ubuntu 10.4.

Services that MangleBracket uses and recommends:

Beanstalk for Subversion
GoDaddy for domain registration and DNS
SliceHost for unmanaged hosting
